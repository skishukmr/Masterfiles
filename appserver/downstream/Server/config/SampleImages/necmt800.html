MultiSync MT800 LCD Projector

The NEC MultiSync MT800 LCD Projector weighs less than 16 pounds and offers users true-800x600 resolution, compressed 1024x768 resolution, as well as windowed or full-screen 648x480 resolution. The flagship of the MT Series, the MT800 is very portable and ideal for traditional corporate boardroom applications as well as remote sales and training, and is priced for educational use at the primary and secondary levels. Offering a tremendously bright 350 ANSI lumen image, the projector provides a power zoom focus lens.
