Technology for the impatient.

At the heart of the Ultra 1 is the 64-bit UltraSPARC ITM microprocessor. Demonstrating the incredible scalability of the SPARCTM architecture, the UltraSPARC ITM combines high processor speeds with an efficient four-way superscalar design for unprecedented desktop performance. Its nine-stage pipeline issues up to four instructions per cycle. In short, it's fast!

To make the most of this powerful processor, our engineers developed the Ultra Port Architecture (UPA), a crossbar-switched interconnect that enables multiple simultaneous data transfers, providing ten times the bandwidth of previous bus-based technologies. It has more memory bandwidth through the double data-width memory subsystem; a faster and wider industry-standard expansion bus, with the 64-bit SBus running at 25 MHz; faster I/O, with Fast/Wide SCSI functionality; and faster networking through the integrated Fast Ethernet port. Data transfers between processor, memory, I/O, and graphics are faster than ever before. Which means vastly improved application performance.

The UltraSPARC processor's revolutionary Visual Instruction Set (VIS) - the industry's most flexible and comprehensive array of on-chip multimedia, graphics, and imaging technologies-delivers the new era of digital multimedia right to your desktop.

The Ultra 1 maintains 100% binary compatibility with Sun's entire product family. You can still run more than 10,000 solutions available on the SPARC/SolarisTM platform faster and better than ever before without recompiling. By leveraging and improving upon the features of the highly successful SPARCstation family, the Ultra 1 inherits and expands upon system modularity, expandability, and configurability-the hallmarks of all SPARCstation designs.

Ethernet connections. If you opt for the entry-level Ultra 1, you can move up to 100-Mb/sec. networking by plugging in a SunFastEthernetTM adaptor.

Ultra 1 Graphics: The power to create.

In addition to the entry-level Ultra 1 170 TGX, cost-effective 8-bit graphics solutions for EDA, CASE, and a wide range of business applications, you can choose either the Ultra 1 Creator or Ultra 1 Creator3D - systems that lift visual computing to a new level of performance.

Creator Graphics gives you 24-bit, single-buffered 2-D graphics and imaging, enabling you to pan, zoom, rotate, convolve, and do color conversion at unprecedented speeds. Creator Graphics, tightly coupled to the processor through the UPA interconnect, allow the entire system to act as a graphics engine, utilizing floating-point processor speed, VIS capabilities, and memory capacity to their fullest. It is the ideal solution for full-color prepress, publishing, medical, GIS, or software development. The Ultra 1 Creator, with 24-bit true-color graphics capability, was designed from the ground up for interactive graphics, imaging, video playback, and real-time information processing.

Creator 3-D offers all that and more: fast image processing, MPEG video decompression of Creator graphics, plus accelerated 24-bit, double- and Z-buffered 3-D rendering. 3D-RAM technology combines the best features of DRAM, SRAM, and VRAM onto one chip-delivering up to 600 million pixel operations per second.The Ultra 1 Creator3D system combines true-color graphics, high-speed image processing, video decompression, 3-D rendering capabilities. Creator 3D's perfect for visualization, MCAD, medical imaging, and molecular modeling.
