TLP-311
LCD Projector
The Toshiba media Star TLP-311 is the world's first LCD Multimedia projector with a built in document camera.
 
With the new Toshiba TLP-311, featuring a built in document camera, you can have everything you've always wanted in a self-contained LCD projector - superior image quality, compact size, and the latest in multimedia technology. Plus you'll have the brightest picture, thanks to Toshiba's exclusive 375 Lumens Optical System.
Just one look and you'll know the TLP-311 is the best and brightest. video and computer images come alive in and incredibly rich palate of more than 16.7 million colors, even in lighted rooms. No other projector can match this brilliance or clarity.
For all it's advance features, the TLP-311 is also the most user friendly LCD on the market. It is compatible with you VCR, camcorder and laser disk, as well as your computers, including the newest Pentium and PowerPCs.
In a hurry? The ultra-lightweight TLP-311 is a breeze to carry. Its compact dimensions allow it to fit under an airplane seat on in an overhead compartment.
