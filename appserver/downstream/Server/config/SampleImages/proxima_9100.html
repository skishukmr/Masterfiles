Description & Benefits
The perfect solution for demanding high-resolution projection. It offers premium performance for training sessions and workgroups as well as for formal presentations. The Fit-to-View feature displays the entire image or the "big picture" all at once, while still providing the ability to show the fine details of that same image. Ideal for projecting CAD/CAM, GIS, command and control, and business graphics images. Intelligent scaling capability projects even the most detailed workstation CAD image with clarity, making it perfect for design reviews or client presentations. The rugged design is a natural fit with portable command and control applications.


User Benefits:
  • Compatible with all popular workstations and PCs, projecting a full range of resolutions
  • Bright images of outstanding quality for high-resolution applications
  • Optional MediaExpress image display system for presentations without the computer, cables, or the worry
