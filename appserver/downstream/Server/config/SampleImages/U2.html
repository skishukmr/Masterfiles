Ultra 2


Product Overview

Supercomputer-Class Computing Power for the Desktop

Sun Ultra 2 is the highest-performing desktop system of the UltraSPARCTM product family. The Ultra 2 system incorporates the latest advances and technology in multiple processing, CPU, graphics, I/O, networking, and memory design. It also offers increased upgradability and expandability relative to the Ultra 1 system.
From 3-D mechanical design to medical imaging, from financial modeling to simulation and analysis, the Sun Ultra 2 system delivers all the power, multiprocessing throughput, I/O, and memory needed for today's demanding technical and business applications. Wherever the highest performance is a must, that is where the Ultra 2 system excels.

The Ultra 2 desktop begins with the 64-bit UltraSPARCTM microprocessor, in a single- or dual-processor configuration. These microprocessors, housed on simple plug-in modules, combine high clock speeds with an efficient four-way superscalar design that yields industry-leading integer and floating-point performance. They are surrounded by balanced technologies that together deliver outstanding performance in even the most compute-intensive applications.

For instance, the UltraSPARC Port Architecture (UPA) interface unleashes the processors' incredible power. Based on a crossbar-switched architecture, this interconnect enables multiple simultaneous data transfers and provides an order of magnitude more bandwidth than previous bus-based technologies. Because data moves between processors, memory, and I/O more efficiently, transfers are faster than ever-giving you greatly increased performance.

And UltraSPARC's revolutionary Visual Instruction Set (VIS) on-chip multimedia, graphics, and imaging technology delivers a whole new world of networked digital media to the desktop.

Plus, with vast amounts of memory capacity at your disposal, you have enough horsepower to drive even the most demanding applications. The Sun Ultra 2 system's fast/wide, 20-MB/sec. SCSI-2 interface delivers accelerated performance from those same applications you depend on most.

The Ultra 2 system is also binary compatible with our entire product family. That means you can take advantage of SolarisTM, the industry's most powerful and popular UNIX®environment, and the more than 10,000 applications that run-and run best-on the SPARCTM/Solaris platform, without recompilation.

What Is New with Ultra 2?



•More configurations

The Ultra 2 system now includes 200-MHz and 300-MHz uni-processor models, and 167-MHz, 200-MHz, and 300-MHz dual-processor models.

•Improved CPU and graphics performance

UPA interconnect speed has been increased from 83 MHz to 100 MHz on 200-MHz and 300-MHz Ultra 2 systems. UPA runs at 83 MHz on 167-MHz Ultra 2 systems, but can be increased to 100 MHz when the system is upgraded to a faster processor. Additionally, in 200-MHz and 300-MHz Ultra 2 systems, faster application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) on Creator3D combined with higher UPA bandwidth raise graphics performance by more than 20 percent over the Creator3D graphics on 167-MHz Ultra 2 systems.

•Greater memory expansion

Ultra 2 systems now support up to 2 GB of system memory using 128-MB SIMMs, up from 1 GB.
