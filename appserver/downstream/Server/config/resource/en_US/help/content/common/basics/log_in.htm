My Supply Cabinet Online Help
    
Tips   Log in

Once logged in, if you are inactive in My Supply Cabinet for a given period of time, as configured by your company, My Supply Cabinet displays a logout warning and begins a logout countdown. To continue working, click Cancel Logout before the countdown ends. To log out, click Logout or wait for the countdown to end, at which point you are logged out automatically.

Screen details

The Log in screen is displayed when you access My Supply Cabinet.

 
  1. Open an internet browser.
  2. Access CAT@Work.
  3. Click My Workspace.
  4. Click on the My Supply Cabinet link under the Work Tools tab.
  5. On the My Supply Cabinet page that appears, click the My Supply Cabinet image to enter the solution.

    If you have the necessary system administrator permission, you can log into My Supply Cabinet and then click Administrator in the Explore section of the Navigation Panel on the My Supply Cabinet Home page. To learn more about using My Supply Cabinet Administrator, see the online help in My Supply Cabinet Administrator.

  6. If you are a system administrator, you may be asked to select a partition and then the Home page or the specified product screen is displayed.

    If you log in as a system administrator, the partition you select determines the objects you can edit in My Supply Cabinet Administrator. You can edit objects only in the login partition; you have read-only access to objects in other partitions. You can also access integration events, scheduled tasks, and rules in all partitions. To ensure you have access to all catalog administration tasks, select the Supplier Direct partition.
