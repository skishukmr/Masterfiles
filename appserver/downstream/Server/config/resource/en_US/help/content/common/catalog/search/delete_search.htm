Screen details

You delete a catalog search from either the Explore Catalog or Add Items screen. Select Options > Saved searches > Other. The screen title remains Explore Catalog or Add Items and your list of saved searches displays.
 
  1. Select Options > Other to view your lists of saved searches.
  2. Check the check box for the search to delete.
  3. Click Delete. The search is permanently deleted.
