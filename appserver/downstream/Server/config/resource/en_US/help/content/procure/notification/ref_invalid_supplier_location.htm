Tips   Notification 16: Invalid Supplier Location

To resolve an invalid supplier location, the error must be corrected in the My Supply Cabinet configuration files. Locate the appropriate supplier contact, either in your ERP system or in the My Supply Cabinet supplier list, and correct the contact information.

About notification messages

Notification messages
 

To help prevent errors when sending orders to suppliers, My Supply Cabinet regularly checks all suppliers in its database to ensure each supplier location has valid contact information. When a problem arises, My Supply Cabinet emails a notification message to users who have the SupplierLocationCheck permission, typically purchasing agents.

The message indicates the problem for each supplier, such as a missing preferred ordering method, email address, or location.
