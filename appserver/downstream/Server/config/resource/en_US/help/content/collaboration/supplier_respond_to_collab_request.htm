Tips   Respond to a collaboration request

When viewing the details of a collaboration request, you can click Respond to take several actions related to the request.

Note: On items that request collaboration on a contractor, the unit price shown on the item summary can be less than the bill rate shown on the item details, because the unit price includes all the discounts to which you are entitled.

Screen details

When viewing collaboration requests in your Inbox, click a request's title to display its details in the Collaboration Status screen.
 
  1. To take actions on the request, click Respond, and then do one of the following:
    • To send a message to this supplier, click Send Message. Alternatively, you can click Send Message in the Last Message area at the top of the screen.
    • To create a proposal for this request, click Respond With Proposal .
    • To decline to provide a proposal for this request, click Decline Request.
    • If you have already created a proposal for this request, and you want to revise it and resend it to the buying organization, click Revise Proposal.
    • To view the message board where you exchange messages about this request with this supplier, click Messages.
    • To view the request in printable format, click Print.
  2. When you are finished viewing the details of the collaboration request, click the Back link at the bottom of the screen.
