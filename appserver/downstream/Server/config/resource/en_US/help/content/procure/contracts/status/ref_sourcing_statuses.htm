Tips   Sourcing request statuses

Some purchase requisition statuses also apply to sourcing requests: Composing, Submitted, Approved, Denied, Ordering, Ordered, Canceling, and Canceled.

Only users with the permission necessary to work with sourcing requests can view SRs and CSRs. To view documents with this status, click the Sourcing status link in the Home page.

Ariba Contract Compliance emails notification messages to keep relevant users informed of the status of sourcing requests, and logs the history onto the History tab.

A spot purchase sourcing request (SR) or a contract sourcing request (CSR). changes status as it moves through the business process.

This status Indicates
Confirming

SRs that have a corresponding sourcing event, or RFX, active in Ariba Enterprise Sourcing. The original purchase request is fully approved, but Ariba Contract Compliance has not yet received trade confirmations from selected suppliers.

Sourced

SRs and CSRs that have a corresponding RFX for which selected suppliers have been chosen. The sourcing request has been updated with information about the selected suppliers.

At this point, for an SR, Ariba Contract Compliance updates the original purchase request with information from the sourcing request and moves the request through the approval process. For a CSR, Ariba Contract Compliance creates a draft contract request (CR) for each selected supplier, which must be completed and submitted for approval.

Sourcing

SRs and CSRs that have a corresponding RFX.

If an SR fails, but there are still other active SRs, the requisition remains in the Sourcing status. However, if an SR fails and there are no other active SRs, the requisition returns to the Submitted status.
The Sourcing status is replaced by the following status updates from Ariba Enterprise Sourcing, to indicate current RFX activity:
  Open SRs and CSRs that have a corresponding RFX open for bidding by invited suppliers.
  Pending Selection SRs and CSRs that have a corresponding RFX that is closed to further bidding, and for which the RFX initiator/owner has been asked to evaluate bids and select winners.
Sourcing Completed

SRs and CSRs that have a corresponding RFX, and for which Ariba Contract Compliance has received trade confirmations from the selected suppliers.

Sourcing Failed

SRs and CSRs that have a corresponding RFX that cannot be processed further. Sourcing requests can fail when a supplier withdraws its winning bid or when a request is canceled or expires.
