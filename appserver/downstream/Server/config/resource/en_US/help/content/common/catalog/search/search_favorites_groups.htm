Tips   Search in a favorites group
When you add an item to a favorites group, a star appears beside it in the catalog to make it easier to see.

When you add a favorite item to a request, My Supply Cabinet automatically updates the item price and description.

Screen details

You search in a favorites group from either the Explore Catalog or the Add Items screen, select Favorites > group name.

 
  1. If the Favorites field does not appear on the Explore Catalog screen or the Add Items screen, click Options to display the pull-down menu and select Custom Favorites. The Favorites field displays.
  2. Select a favorite group from the Favorites pull-down menu. All the items in the group display.
  3. Enter one or two keyword(s) to describe the item, add more search options if desired, and enter the additional search criteria.
  4. Click Search.
    • Items in the favorites group that meet your search criteria display.
    • A lock displays beside the favorite group and the group name highlights to indicate that your search is confined to items inside the group.
  5. Click the lock to unlock the group and reset the screen.
