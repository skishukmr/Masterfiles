Tips   Delete a document

When you delete a request that you are composing, My Supply Cabinet removes the record permanently.

To skip confirming the deletion, click the appropriate check box in the confirmation screen. Reset your default preferences to restore screens you've chosen to hide.

Screen details

To display the Confirm Delete screen, click Delete for one or more selected documents in a list of documents, such as requests or reports.
 
  1. Select one or more documents to delete by clicking the associated check boxes and then Delete.
  2. Click Yes to confirm the deletion, or click No to cancel the deletion and return to the previous screen.
