Tips   View catalog item details

My Supply Cabinet contains screens that are used to display the details of a specific value, such as a supplier or a catalog item.

Catalog details can include product description, kit information (for catalog kits), supplier, supplier part number, manufacturer, manufacturing part number, estimated time of arrival in days, and information from the supplier and manufacturer websites (when available).

An item's location in the catalog is displayed in the breadcrumb at the bottom of the screen.

Screen details

To view catalog item details, display an item on the Explore Catalog or Add Items screen and click the item's link.
 

After you search for catalog items, click any item's link to display additional details about the item, such as product or supplier details.

  • To view additional information about an item, click any item-related link in the Product Details area. If the supplier is Unspecified, the supplier details screen will not contain any information.
  • To add the catalog item to your shopping cart, click Add to Cart. If the item contains fields that must be specified before ordering, you must fill in the blank fields before adding the item to your shopping cart.
  • To add the item to a favorites group or remove the item from an existing favorites group, click Add to Favorites.
  • To return to the previous screen, click back.
  • To see the item's location in the catalog, click the category links near the bottom of the screen.
