Orders reports

These reports focus on items being ordered, the dollars being committed, and the suppliers being used. They help you understand how your money is being allocated.

This report Displays
Order Summary by Supplier (all partitions) A summary of orders based on supplier and commodity. The orders are for requisitions whose status is Ordered or Shipped.
Order Details by Commodity and Supplier A summary of ordered items. This report helps you identify the kinds of items being ordered by your company, and from which suppliers.
Orders Overview An overview of orders by supplier, including number of orders placed with a supplier, the average order amount, and the total order amount.
Orders by Commodity and Cost Center Items ordered by your company, grouped by commodity code and cost center.
Order Summary by Commodity (all partitions) A summary of all commodities ordered from specific suppliers. You can filter results by commodity code, supplier, and ordered date. The orders are for requisitions whose status is Ordered or Shipped.
Order Summary by Cost Center A list of the cost centers to which items are charged. This is an administrative report that helps you filter results by cost center, supplier, and ordered date.
Order Summary by Supplier A summary of orders based on supplier, cost center, and commodity. The orders are for requisitions whose status is Ordered or Shipped.
PCard Orders by Card Number

A summary of purchasing card (PCard) transactions for a specific time period. Like a billing statement from the credit card company, this report shows a history of purchases within a given time period. The report also includes transactions for all available registered card numbers, and is a useful for reconciling PCard expense reports.

PCard Orders by Requester

A summary, similar to a billing statement from a credit card company, of items purchased using a PCard. Most reports are used by purchasing card administrators who need a broad perspective on spending and ordering patterns. However, this report is useful for tracking your own PCard purchases.
Requisition Total by Supplier The total dollar amount of items ordered, by supplier, for requisitions whose status is Ordered or Shipped. This report helps you see frequently used suppliers, and possibly negotiate better prices with them.
Supplier Summary

Contact information, such as primary contact person and email address, for each supplier in your company's supplier directory.
