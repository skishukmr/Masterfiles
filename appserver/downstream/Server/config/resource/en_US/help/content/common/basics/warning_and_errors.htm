Tips   About My Supply Cabinet errors or warnings

Warnings are informational and typically do not require any action on your part. Errors typically require you to take an action.

If you are inactive in My Supply Cabinet for a given period of time, as configured by your company, My Supply Cabinet displays a logout warning and begins a logout countdown. To continue working, click Cancel Logout before the countdown ends. To log out, click Logout or wait for the countdown to end, at which point you are logged out automatically.

Screen details

The Warning or Error screen is displayed if an action that you've taken results in a warning or an error.
 

My Supply Cabinet can display a warning in response to certain actions. When this occurs, read the warning and click the appropriate button, for example to continue to the next step of the current task or to return to the previous step.

My Supply Cabinet can also display an error in response to certain actions. When this occurs, follow any suggestions provided to correct the error.
