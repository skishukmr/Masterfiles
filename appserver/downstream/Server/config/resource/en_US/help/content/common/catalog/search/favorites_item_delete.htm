Tips   Delete a favorite item from a group

When you add an item to a favorites group, a star appears beside it in the catalog to make it easier to see.

  • As long as the item remains in a favorites group, the star continues to display.
  • The star disappears when you remove the favorite item from all associated favorites group(s).

Screen details

You delete favorites items from a group from either the Explore Catalog or the Add Items screen. To delete favorite items, click Add to Favorites next to a favorite catalog item and then choose Other.
 
  1. Find the item in the catalog or open the favorites group to display the item.
  2. Click the Add to Favorites link.
  3. Select the group that is checked to remove the item from the group.
