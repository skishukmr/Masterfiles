Return On Investment (ROI) reports

These reports show the time and money you spend processing requests. They can show where your process is working well, and highlight areas where you might improve the process to maximize the benefits of using My Supply Cabinet. For example, the reports might reveal that some types of requests take longer to process, indicating that a change in process could reduce delays.

This report Displays
Requisition Average Cycle Time Analysis The average number of days required to approve requests within a specified time period, with requests grouped by amount. Typically, requests with lower amounts take less approval time because fewer people are involved in the approval process. If the report indicates otherwise, you might want to reevaluate the criteria on which approval flows are based, or change the monetary limits that trigger approvals.
Requisition Volume Over Time The total amount spent every month, followed by a summary of the average amount of each request. Use this report to reveal trends in how you purchase goods over a period of time. The report is also useful for determining My Supply Cabinet usage. For example, you can compare the amount spent each month using My Supply Cabinet against the total amount spent, to determine the percentage of request volume being sent through My Supply Cabinet.
Catalog vs. Non-Catalog Orders A summary of the amount spent on catalog items and non-catalog items during a specified time period. This report helps you evaluate whether a catalog meets your needs. For example, if more money is consistently spent on non-catalog items, and if the same non-catalog items are consistently ordered, you can arrange to have those items added to a catalog to reduce costs.
