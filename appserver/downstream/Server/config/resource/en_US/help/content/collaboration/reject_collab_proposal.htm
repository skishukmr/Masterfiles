Tips   Reject a collaboration proposal or candidate

At any time during collaboration, you can reject a collaboration proposal or just a candidate on a proposal from a supplier. When you reject a proposal or candidate, an email notification is sent to the supplier.

Screen details

To display the Rejecting Proposal screen, click the supplier name in the proposal, and then choose Reject Proposal from the pull-down list. To display the Reject Candidate screen, click the candidate name in the proposal details, and then choose Reject from the pull-down list.

 
  1. In the Reject Reason list, select a reason for rejecting this proposal or candidate.
  2. Optionally, enter a further explanation of why you are rejecting the proposal or candidate.
  3. Click OK.

To undo rejection of a proposal, click the supplier name, and then choose Withdraw Rejection from the pull-down menu. To undo rejection of a candidate, click the candidate name, and then choose Clear Status from the pull-down menu.
