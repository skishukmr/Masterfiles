Tips   Act as another user

If another user has delegated approval authority to you, My Supply Cabinet indicates you can log in as that user, and how many requests are awaiting your approval.

Related topics

Continue or stop approval delegation

Delegate approval authority

 
Screen details

The Act as User screen is displayed when you log into My Supply Cabinet while another user has delegated approval authority to you.
 

Click the name of the user whose approval authority you have been delegated, and view the requests awaiting your approval.

You can also return to the Home page or click Logout.
