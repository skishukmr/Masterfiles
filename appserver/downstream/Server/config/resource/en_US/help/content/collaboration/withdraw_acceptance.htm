Tips   Withdraw acceptance of a collaboration proposal

At any time during the collaboration phase, you can withdraw acceptance of a collaboration proposal you previously accepted. After collaboration ends, however, you can no longer withdraw acceptance of the proposal. Instead, you must take action on the requisition or order, depending on the status. For example, if the requisition status is Submitted, you can withdraw the requisition, but if the status is Ordered, you have to create a change order.

Screen details

To display the Withdraw Acceptance of Proposal screen, click the supplier name, or click the Actions button when viewing the proposal details, and then choose Withdraw Acceptance.

 
  1. Verify the identifier of the proposal for which you are withdrawing acceptance.
  2. Optionally, enter an explanation of why you are withdrawing your acceptance of the proposal.
  3. Click OK.
