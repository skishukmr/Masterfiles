Tips   Select a charge to reconcile

Selecting a charge to reconcile is the first step in categorizing the charge (as a business expense, for example), and assigning it to the appropriate order or expense report.

Screen details

To display the Select Charges screen, click PCard or Expense Card on the Reconcile Charges screen.
 
  1. Review the charges:
    • To view details about a charge, click the merchant name.
    • To search for a charge, click Search to display search fields, enter search text, and click Search again. The search results appear in the lower half of the screen.
    • To assign a charge to a category, click the charge's check box.
  2. Click Next to continue with the reconciliation, or Exit to return to the previous screen.
