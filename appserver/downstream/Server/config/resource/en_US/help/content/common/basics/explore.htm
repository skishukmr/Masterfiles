Tips   Explore searches, catalogs, folders, payments, contracts, or Administrator

Folders are useful for organizing requests and other documents in My Supply Cabinet, while searches help you locate requests or other documents. Search catalogs to find items to order.

Screen details

To display the Explore screen, click Explore in the "swoosh" or central image on the Home page.
 
  • To explore documents by searching, click Searches.
  • To explore catalogs and begin creating a request, click Catalogs.
  • To explore items organized in folders, click Folders.
  • To explore payments, click Payments. This option is displayed only if you have the necessary permission to work with payments. (The Payment functionality will be implemented in Release 2.)
  • To explore contracts to which you have access, click Contracts. This option is displayed only if you have the necessary permission to work with contracts.
  • To go to My Supply Cabinet Administrator, click Administrator. This option is displayed only if you have the necessary system administrator permission.
