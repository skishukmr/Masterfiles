Tips   About non-required approvals

Your company has chosen to automatically approve User Profile changes.

Related topics

Requisition statuses
 

Some requests, such as User Profile changes, are automatically approved and don't require explicit approval. When you click Submit, they move directly from Composing to Approved status.

When a request is automatically approved:

  • It appears in your Approved request list with a status of Approved
  • The Approval Flow tab indicates that no approvals were required
  • The History tab indicates the request was submitted and no approval was required
