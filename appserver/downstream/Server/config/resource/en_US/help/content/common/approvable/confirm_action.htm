Tips   Confirm an action

Confirmation screens appear when you are ready to take permanent action on a request, such as modify or save a request.

Buttons may vary, depending on the screen, and some screens use links instead of buttons.

Related topics

Set preferences

 
Screen details

The appropriate confirmation screen is displayed wherever a permanent action is about to be taken on a document.

 
  • Click Yes or OK (as applicable) to confirm that you want to proceed.
  • Click Cancel or No (as applicable) to return to the previous screen without changing the request.
  • Click the check box to hide the confirmation screen in future. You can restore any confirmation screens you've chosen to hide by resetting your default preferences.
