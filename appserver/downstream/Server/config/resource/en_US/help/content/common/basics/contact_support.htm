Contact Technical Support

Use this page to provide your users with information they need to contact your internal technical support group. Your users can access this page by clicking the Contact Support link located at the bottom of the online help Contents page. The link is disabled by default; you must enable the link to make it visible to your users.

To enable the link to your internal technical support contact information:

  1. Open ariba.css in /branding/config for edit.
  2. Search for the style .supportLink. It appears as:

    /*  remove the first (display) attribute to make a link to an internal support page visible in your online help */
    .supportLink {
        display:none;
        color:#000000;
        font:11px/15px Arial, Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif;
        margin:0px;

  3. Delete the  display:none; attribute. The style should now appear as:

    /*  remove the first (display) attribute to make a link to an internal support page visible in your online help */
    .supportLink {
        color:#000000;
        font:11px/15px Arial, Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif;
        margin:0px;

  4. Save your changes.
  5. Replace all of the default text in this page with your instructions for your users about how to contact your internal technical support group.
